Video Game
-Basic 2d scroller with random level generation and monster/loot spawns
-Jobs
  * Display interface (display images, play sounds)
  * Control interface (WASD, turn around animations, jump physics)
  * Fighting interface (define weapons/enemies, Damage/Health Bar)
  * Map Generation (randomly generate map, random enemy/loot spawns)
  * Asset Creation (sprites/sounds)
  * Background Character Tracking (keep track of loot/levels)
